About Dunk sb

Nike Dunk SB stands for skateboard. The origin of DUNK is about basketball. In the 1990s, NBA basketball players often wore the DUNK style to play basketball. With the progress of technology, the DUNK style was no longer suitable for high-intensity professional basketball, and then it was evolved into a casual shoe style by Nike. Dunk SB is a spin-off of Dunk, which is Nike's attempt to break into the skateboard series in 2000. In 2002, it launched the first Dunk SB, which is popular with skateboarders because of its thick tongue and the technology of air cushion under the insole.
